FAQ Section

Headline: Questions? We're here.

Subtext: Everything you need to know about Noah. Can't find your answer? We'd love to hear from you.

Q: Who is Noah designed for?
Noah is designed primarily for older adults and their caregivers. The interface uses large text (minimum 16px body, 18-20px for buttons), generous tap targets (56-72px), haptic feedback, and high contrast options. No passwords are needed — just your name to log back in.
Q: How does the voice feature work?
Noah uses a tap-to-talk interface — press the microphone button, speak naturally, and Noah responds with a warm, human-like voice powered by AWS Polly's generative engine. The response streams sentence-by-sentence so you hear the first part immediately. You can say things like "Add my new medication" or "What's my schedule today" and Noah takes action directly.
Q: What can Noah actually do from my voice?
Noah is powered by Claude Sonnet AI and can execute real actions from your voice: add or edit medications, set reminders, schedule meditation sessions, add doctor consultations, manage emergency contacts, and mark medications as taken. She understands natural language — no special commands needed.
Q: How does doctor visit recording work?
When you're at a doctor's appointment, tap the record button. Noah captures the conversation hands-free, automatically transcribes it, and generates an AI-powered summary. You can review past consultations, listen to recordings, and share summaries with family members.
Q: Can my family stay connected?
Yes! Family members can set up Noah for their loved ones and receive daily check-in notifications. You can configure trusted contacts with phone and email, and the emergency calling feature is always one tap away. The app is designed to be set up by a tech-savvy family member and used independently.
Q: What about my privacy and data?
Your health data stays on your device by default. We use end-to-end encryption for any data that syncs. We never sell personal information and you can delete all your data at any time from Settings. Doctor recordings and transcriptions are stored securely on your device.
Q: Does it work without internet?
Core features like medication reminders, your agenda, and emergency calling work offline. Voice chat and AI agent features require an internet connection, but your medication schedule, doctor recordings, and contact list are always available even without signal.
Q: What languages does Noah support?
Noah currently supports English and Spanish with full interface translation and native-sounding voices (Ruth for English, Lucia for Spanish via AWS Polly). More languages are planned. You can switch languages anytime from Settings.
